# Playedd — curiosity, retention & comment engineering

Comments are a ranking signal AND social proof. Every short should deliberately trigger one. Three layers:

## 1. Curiosity (first 1-2s) — open a loop the brain must close
- Lead with the weird PAYOFF, not the setup ("Wombats poop cubes." not "Wombats are interesting animals.").
- Ask the question they never thought to ask ("Why is your keyboard not in ABC order?").
- Contradict an assumption ("A mirror doesn't flip left-right.").
- Promise a worth-it payoff ("The reason is stranger than you think.").
- NEVER open with "did you know" — it signals "skippable fact."

## 2. Retention (middle) — don't let them drop
- Escalate: each beat a bigger surprise than the last.
- Mid-roll re-hook at the natural drop point: "But here's the twist…" / "and there's a reason…".
- Make it about the viewer: "You do this every day." Self-recognition holds attention.
- Keep <25s. One word captions (already standard).

## 3. Comment triggers (the close) — END ON ONE, not a flat kicker
Pick ONE per short and make it the final spoken/captioned line OR the pinned comment:
- **"It's not just me?" confirmation** → "Comment if you did this today."
- **A direct question** → "What did you procrastinate on today?" / "Which one are you?"
- **Pick a side (binary)** → "Genius or evil? 👇" — lowest-effort comment there is.
- **Guess / fill-the-blank** → "There's a word for this. Bet you can't guess it."
- **Debate bait** → leave a little nuance room; a mild hot take invites "well actually".
- **Try-it-now challenge** → "Try it right now and tell me it doesn't break your brain."
- **Be-featured loop** → "Drop a 'wait, that's true?' fact — I might make it a short." (the standard tribe loop)

## How we apply it
- Scripts: end on the KICKER, then the PIN carries the comment trigger (keeps the video clean, drives comments below). For some shorts, end the script itself on a question/challenge.
- Pinned comment formula = [bonus jaw-drop tied to the video] → [ONE comment trigger from above] → [Become a Stan / daily promise]. See brand/PINNED_COMMENTS.md.
- Reply FAST to early comments (first hour) and heart them — replies beget replies.
- When a commenter's fact becomes a short, credit them on screen → everyone else starts commenting facts.
