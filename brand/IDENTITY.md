# Playedd — IDENTITY (the north star, locked 2026-06)

Converged after triangulating Claude (analytics + production) and ChatGPT (positioning). This governs EVERY topic decision, short and long.

## North star
**"The hidden why behind being YOU."**
Not a science channel. Not a curiosity channel. A channel about the viewer's own mind, behavior, and existence. Closest competitor Zenn promises "a fascinating truth about humanity"; Playedd promises **"a hidden truth about YOU."** Our moat is not the topic space (it overlaps Zenn) — it's VOICE + EXECUTION: doodle-Stan warmth, eerie-but-comforting tone, and community ("the Stans").

## The product is a FEELING, not information
We are not teaching. We are manufacturing a feeling. Every video must strongly trigger **at least two** of these four emotions:
- 🧠 **Recognition** — "I do that."
- 😳 **Discomfort** — "that's unsettling."
- 🤯 **Wonder** — "I never thought about that."
- 🫥 **Existential unease** — "wait… who am I then?"
If it doesn't hit at least two, don't make it. By format: **shorts lean Recognition + Wonder** (light, shareable); **flagships add Discomfort + Existential unease** (the prestige tier).

## The one rule
**No topic can be purely external.** Every topic must point back to the viewer.
- ❌ Sharks Are Older Than Trees → ✅ Sharks Are Older Than Trees (and Why Your Brain Can't Process That)
- ❌ Dead Stars → ✅ You Are Literally Made Of Dead Stars
- ❌ Childhood Amnesia → ✅ Your Brain Deleted Your First Years
Steal frameworks, never topics. Same concept, fresh angle.

## The pillars (target mix)
- **45% Hidden Brain** — your mind: cringe memories, time speeding up, childhood amnesia, replaying embarrassing moments.
- **25% Hidden Behavior** — things you do daily: awkward silences, procrastination, mirroring people, interrupting.
- **20% Hidden Society** — things we built: weekends, schools, offices, money.
- **10% Hidden Existence** — the big ones (FLAGSHIP tier, posted rarely, define the channel): why life feels short, loneliness, fear of death, consciousness.

## The topic scorecard (the gate)
Score each question 2 pts yes / 0 no:
1. Is this secretly about the viewer?
2. Does it create an identity crisis?
3. Is there an "I never thought about that" moment?
4. Is there a visual people immediately understand?
5. Could it support an 8-minute deep dive?

- **0–4 → discard**
- **5–7 → short only**
- **8–10 → long-form candidate (flagship)**
This dovetails with the operating system: shorts test topics; analytics + a high score promote a topic to long-form. In practice the GUT question leads ("did I feel ≥2 emotions?"); the scorecard is the short-vs-flagship tiebreaker — don't fill out a form per video.

## Pipeline order (topic quality is the ceiling)
TOPIC → TITLE → THUMBNAIL → SCRIPT → VISUAL EXECUTION. A great title cannot rescue a mediocre topic. Packaging is the #1 *lever* but only AFTER topic selection.
