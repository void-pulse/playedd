# Stan — the Playedd everyman (recurring character)

Stan is the channel's recurring stick-figure everyman: the ordinary person the world quietly happens to. He's the "you" in every episode. Introduced ep6 ("Why Are You Lazy?"). Use him whenever an episode needs a human example.

## Why he exists
- **Relatability + continuity.** A familiar face across episodes makes the channel feel like a place, not a pile of one-offs, and turns abstract science into "that's me." Returning viewers recognize him.
- He's the everyman version of the Playedd logo doodle.

## Visual spec (keep consistent — this is the whole point)
- A simple, crude hand-drawn stick figure in the channel STYLE_BLOCK (brand/IMAGE_STYLE.md): round head, dot or small-circle eyes, simple line body, basic expressive face.
- Same look every time so he's instantly recognizable. Don't redesign him per episode.
- Neutral/relatable, not a hero or a clown. Expressive enough to carry a feeling (tired, guilty, curious, relieved) with two lines.
- He's silent. The narration is about him and you, never voiced by him.

## How to use him
- Open on a small, concrete, relatable Stan scenario (ep6: "Stan had one thing to do, go for a run, and didn't"). That's the real-life hook.
- Bring him back through the episode as the running example, and call back to him in the button.
- He can age/dress to fit a scene (caveman Stan, etc.) but stays recognizably Stan.
- One per frame still applies: Stan + one bold idea, not Stan buried in clutter.

## Name
**Stan** (stick-man + everyman). Locked 2026-06-08.
