# Playedd Script Playbook — how to write an episode (for autonomous builds)

This is the recipe for writing a Playedd script at the channel's quality bar without a human in the loop. Follow it with brand/STORY.md (spine), VOICE.md (register), FORMATS.md (format), CHARACTER.md (Stan), IMAGE_STYLE.md (visuals). Worked examples: episodes ep5-ep10.

## 0. Pick the topic
Take the next un-built episode from the PRODUCTION QUEUE in `content/ideas/wonder_slate.md`. Keep vein rotation (never the same vein twice in a row). Never reuse a topic Zenn already owns (see zenn_teardown.md).

## 1. Research FIRST, then write (non-negotiable)
- Web-search the load-bearing facts before drafting. Verify every number, name, date, and claim.
- **Honesty is the moat.** If the popular version of a fact is overstated or wrong, say so and give the truer, stranger version (that correction is often the best beat — see ep10 Libet, ep-bank "blue").
- Never state a contested claim as fact. Flag debated points and present them honestly.
- Put real SOURCES at the bottom of the script, and a REGISTER + MYTH-RISK FLAGS block (what not to overstate).

## 2. The register (this is what makes it Playedd)
- **Neutral + wonder.** Leave the viewer with "huh, that's amazing," nothing else. Curiosity, not cynicism.
- **Never make the viewer feel bad about themselves.** No insecurity-poking, no shaming, no doom. If a topic is a "flaw" (lazy, procrastinate, tired), reframe it as a fascinating MECHANISM ("you're not lazy, your brain saves energy"), which lands reassuring, not as a pep talk.
- Subject can be personal/human; emotion stays neutral. (Don't conflate "human subject" with "emotional manipulation.")
- For heavy-adjacent topics (death, mortality, the self), land on AWE or gentle liberation, never nihilism or distress.
- No woo/mysticism, keep it concrete (studies, mechanisms, history).

## 3. Structure (map to brand/STORY.md spine)
COLD OPEN (hook) -> SETUP/stakes -> THE TURN -> escalating reveals -> [HUMAN LAYER] -> PAYOFF -> BUTTON.
- **Hook in the first 2 seconds.** Open inside the mystery on the most arresting line. **Never give the answer away in the cold open** — pose the question, tease that the answer is stranger than expected, reveal it across the video.
- **Head-fake** where it fits: open on the "obvious" answer, then break it (the real one is better). One per episode.
- **The reframe:** the "it's not X, it's Y" flip is the shareable payoff.
- **History thread:** when the topic supports it, weave in the fun/surprising history (Logan loves this — see ep6 sloth/acedia, ep10 Egyptians/Aristotle). Make it a real beat, not a footnote.
- **BUTTON:** twist it onto the viewer, end on wonder. No spoken brand slogan; cut to the silent logo.

## 4. Stan
Use Stan (brand/CHARACTER.md) as the everyman: open on a small, concrete, relatable Stan scenario, bring him back through as the example, call back in the button. Keep him consistent.
- **Use his name sparingly (Logan note 2026-06-10).** Establish him early, bring him back at key beats and the button — but don't say "Stan" every other sentence. "He," "you," or an unnamed everyman moment is usually enough. Over-naming reads as overkill.

## 5. Length + craft
- **~1,350-1,450 words (~8 min)** at Jack's locked 0.92x read. (~1,750 words runs ~10 min, too long.) Substance over padding; trim if a render exceeds ~9 min.
- Short sentences, spoken style, fragments OK. **Minimize em dashes** (AI tell). Numbers spelled for the read where natural.

## 6. Files to produce per episode
- `00_idea.md` (vein, working title, hook, reframe, register, verified facts, myth-risk, 3 thumbnail concepts, 4 teaser-short angles)
- `01_script.md` (the narration, tagged beats, SOURCES, REGISTER + MYTH-RISK FLAGS)
- `02_storyboard.md` (beat anchors, one bold idea per frame, Stan featured, thumbnail concepts)
- `03_worker_brief.md` (the build order — copy the ep10 brief and swap specifics)

## 7. Critic pass (run on every script before building)
Specific fixes only, never "looks good." Check: hook lands in 2s and withholds the answer; every line earns the next; register holds (neutral wonder, no shame/doom/woo); facts verified + contested ones flagged; head-fake intact; Stan consistent; ~7 min length; minimize em dashes. Fix, then build.
