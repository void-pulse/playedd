We call it fossil fuel, so you probably picture dead dinosaurs, squashed underground into oil. It is one of the most believed myths out there. The truth is way stranger. The oil in your car mostly came from tiny ocean creatures. Plankton and algae, trillions of them, that died and sank to the sea floor millions of years ago. Mud buried them, and heat and pressure slowly cooked them into oil. Coal is different. That one came from ancient swamp forests that fell and never fully rotted. Dinosaurs barely contributed anything. So you are not driving on dinosaurs. You are driving on ancient pond scum.
