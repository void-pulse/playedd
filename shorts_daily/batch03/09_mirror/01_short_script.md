[DAILY SHORT — 09_mirror]
Why does a mirror flip left and right, but never up and down? Here is the twist. It does not flip left to right at all. A mirror flips front to back. When you raise your right hand, your reflection raises the hand on the very same side. Your brain just assumes the figure turned around to face you, so it calls it left and right. The mirror is not reversing you. You are.
