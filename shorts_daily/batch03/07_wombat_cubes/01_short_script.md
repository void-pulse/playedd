[DAILY SHORT — 07_wombat_cubes]
Wombats poop in perfect little cubes. Not rounded, not random. Actual cubes. The walls of their intestines have stiff and stretchy patches that mold each one into six flat sides. And there is a reason. Wombats stack their cube poop on rocks and logs to mark their territory. Round poop would roll away. Cubes stay put. It is the only animal on Earth that does this.
