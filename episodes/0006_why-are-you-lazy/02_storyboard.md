# Storyboard — Why Are You Lazy?

Creative direction for the image track. Worker expands into per-timestamp scenes after audio is locked, following these anchors. Stan (brand/CHARACTER.md) is the everyman throughout, keep him consistent.

## Style (non-negotiable)
- STYLE_BLOCK from brand/IMAGE_STYLE.md (crude flat colored doodle, thick wobbly lines, no 3D/shading). Colored simple scenes (the locked look), one bold idea per frame, lots of breathing room.
- Stan = the same simple stick figure every frame (round head, dot eyes, expressive with two lines).
- 16:9 main; ~1 image / 2.3s, holds on slow lines, quick cuts on fast ones.

## Beat anchors (key frames, Stan featured)

**[COLD OPEN] Stan's failed run**
- Stan melted into a couch, night out the window, a pair of running shoes sitting untouched by the door. A little guilt cloud over his head. One idea: "Stan didn't."
- Cut: a sticky note "GO FOR A RUN" with the box un-ticked.

**[SETUP] can't launch**
- Stan staring at a glowing floating TASK bubble he can't reach, a red "?" over him. Wanting to want to do it.
- Cut: a red X over a stick figure flexing "lazy = flaw?" then question it.

**[THE TURN] the brain is an energy accountant**
- Stan's head opened to show a tiny accountant doodle at a desk guarding a stack of energy "coins" (calories). Label: "SAVE ENERGY."
- Cut: caveman Stan by a fire, calm, conserving, while a saber-tooth lurks at the edge. Burning energy = danger.

**[ESCALATE] the cost-benefit vote**
- A simple balance scale: on one side the RUN (heavy effort), on the other a faint far-off "HEALTHY LATER." Brain stamps "NOT WORTH IT."
- Cut: the same caveman instinct (a dotted line) connecting cave-Stan to couch-Stan. Same wiring, fridge full now.

**[HISTORY TURN] the noonday demon**
- A desert, harsh noon sun, a lone monk sitting; a small red cartoon "demon" hovering at his shoulder. Label: "ACEDIA — the noonday demon."

**[ESCALATE HISTORY] sloth, a deadly sin**
- A list labeled "8" with a hand crossing it down to "7"; one line glowing: "SLOTH." A red "DEADLY SIN" stamp.

**[SECOND HISTORY TURN] the factory rebrand**
- A factory with a giant clock on the wall, tiny Stan beneath it; a banner "LIVING TO WORK." A couch with a red "SIN" stamp slapped on it. Rest = wasted time.

**[HUMAN LAYER] Stan in the middle**
- Stan in the center; on his left a caveman-instinct figure ("SURVIVE"), on his right a monk + factory clock ("GUILT"). They're pulling at him. Big calm caption: "NOTHING WRONG WITH YOU."

**[PAYOFF] shrink the first step**
- Stan facing one enormous first stair labeled "START," looking up at it; then it breaks into tiny easy steps. "THE FIRST STEP COSTS THE MOST."

**[BUTTON] old software**
- Stan back on the couch, but calm and knowing now, a little thought bubble showing tiny gears labeled "VERY OLD SOFTWARE." At peace, not guilty.
- Then: end card (main video) = silent like/subscribe doodle, LOWER-LEFT only, center + top-right kept clear (brand/FORMATS.md). No CTA text on the main video.

## Thumbnail (one bold idea)
- Stan sunk deep into a couch, limbs flopped, big bold caps "LAZY?" + a small red "?". High-contrast solid background. One idea. (Establishes Stan as the face of the series.)
