# Animated Short Spec — "Why Are You Lazy?" (reframe teaser) — ANIMATION A/B TEST

The first fully-animated Stan short. This is the **animated version of ep6 teaser short #1 (the reframe)**, built to A/B against the static version: do the same beat both ways, publish, see if motion lifts views. Highest-ROI place to test animation (shorts feed rewards motion; shorts are our discovery engine).

## Format
- Native **9:16 vertical**, ~20-24s.
- Pipeline: doodle **stills (portrait)** → **Kling image-to-video** per shot (standard or Pro, **1080p, NOT 4K**, flat doodles gain nothing from 4K) → stitch with **Jack VO** + light score, full-bleed.
- Stan on-model every shot (brand/CHARACTER.md). Neutral-wonder, reassuring, never shaming.
- **Withhold the payoff** (no monk / sin / factory). End on the "?" itch.
- End card (short standard): **Playedd logo + up-arrow + "FOR FULL BREAKDOWN."**
- No lip-sync needed (Stan's mouth is a line; gesture + VO sells it).

## VO (Jack) — ~60 words
"Meet Stan. Stan had all day to go for one run. He didn't. And now he feels like garbage about it. But here's the thing: Stan isn't lazy. His brain is a survival machine, built over millions of years to never waste energy. Doing nothing is what kept your ancestors alive. So why does it feel like a crime? That part is way stranger than you think."

## Shot list (each = a portrait still → Kling motion, ~3s each, cut to the VO)
1. **"Meet Stan. Stan had all day to go for one run."** — Stan standing by the front door, running shoes in hand, looking at them. *Motion:* he weighs the shoes, then lowers them / shrugs.
2. **"He didn't. And now he feels like garbage about it."** — Stan flopping backward onto the couch, defeated, a little guilt cloud popping over his head. *Motion:* the comedic flop onto the couch + cloud appears. (the money shot)
3. **"But here's the thing: Stan isn't lazy."** — Stan on the couch, perking up slightly, a question mark over him. *Motion:* small head-tilt / perk-up.
4. **"His brain is a survival machine, built to never waste energy."** — Stan's head opens to a tiny accountant-brain guarding a stack of energy "coins" (calories). *Motion:* the little brain hugs/guards the coins.
5. **"Doing nothing is what kept your ancestors alive."** — caveman Stan resting calmly by a fire, a saber-tooth lurking at the edge. *Motion:* fire flickers, caveman Stan stays put, calm.
6. **"So why does it feel like a crime? That part is way stranger than you think."** — Stan back on the couch beside a glowing red "GUILT?" / "?", intrigued. *Motion:* Stan shrug + the red "?" pops in.
7. **End card** — Playedd logo + up-arrow + "FOR FULL BREAKDOWN" (static, ~2s).
