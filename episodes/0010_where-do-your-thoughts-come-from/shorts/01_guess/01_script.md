# ep10 short 1 — guess your next thought (teaser)

[SHORT]

Try something with me. Guess your next thought. Not think it. Guess it, before it arrives.

You can't, can you? It just shows up, on its own, fully formed, like it wandered in from another room.

So if you didn't write that thought, who did?

The answer changes how the voice in your head sounds forever.
