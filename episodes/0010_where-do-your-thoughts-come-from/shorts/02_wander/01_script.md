# ep10 short 2 — 47% mind-wandering (teaser)

[SHORT]

Right now, part of your mind is somewhere else. And it happens way more than you would ever guess.

Harvard researchers pinged thousands of people at random through their day, asking where their mind was.

The answer: wandering about forty-seven percent of the time. Nearly half your waking life.

Carried around by thoughts you never chose. So who is steering, if it isn't you?
