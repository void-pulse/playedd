# ep10 short 4 — the 2am thought (teaser)

[SHORT]

That cruel little thought that ambushes you at 2am. The one you would never say out loud.

Here's the part worth knowing. You didn't write it.

It arrived on its own, from a process you do not control. The same way you can't stop thinking of a white bear once I say it.

So maybe you don't have to believe it. And maybe it was never really yours.
