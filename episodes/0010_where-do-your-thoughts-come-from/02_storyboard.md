# Storyboard — Where Do Your Thoughts Come From?

Creative direction for the image track. Worker expands into per-timestamp scenes after audio is locked, following these anchors. Stan (brand/CHARACTER.md) is the everyman noticing his own mind, consistent every frame.

## Style (non-negotiable)
- STYLE_BLOCK from brand/IMAGE_STYLE.md (crude flat colored doodle, thick wobbly lines, no 3D/shading). Colored simple scenes (locked look), ONE bold idea per frame, breathing room. 16:9; ~1 image / 2.3s.

## Beat anchors (key frames, Stan featured)

**[COLD OPEN] guess your next thought**
- Stan scrunching his face, trying to predict his next thought (a blank thought bubble + "?"). Cut: a random SANDWICH pops into the bubble out of nowhere. Caption: "WHO SENT THAT?"

**[SETUP] uninvited thoughts**
- Stan standing calm while thought-bubbles drift in unasked: a music note, a little worry cloud, a cringe memory. He didn't invite any of them.

**[THE WHITE BEAR] you can't stop one either**
- Stan told "DON'T think of a white bear" (a no-symbol over a bear). Cut: a big goofy white bear bursts right into his thought bubble anyway. Caption: "CAN'T STOP IT."

**[THE TURN] reader, not writer**
- Split: Stan at a keyboard with the chair empty (he's NOT typing) vs Stan in a chair reading a screen as a thought scrolls onto it. Caption: "YOU'RE READING THEM, NOT WRITING THEM."

**[REVEAL 1] the default mode network**
- Stan in the shower as a bright lightbulb idea pops over him ("shower thoughts"). Cut: Stan's body at a desk while a faint ghost-Stan floats off daydreaming, tag "47% OF THE TIME."

**[REVEAL 2] the brain decides first (Libet)**
- Stan watching a big clock, hand ready to flick. A glow lights up in his head FIRST, then a beat later an "I DECIDED!" bubble. A little timeline: BRAIN -> (a third of a second) -> "I CHOSE." Plus a small hand hitting a "VETO" button (free won't). Keep it clear it's debated (a small "?" tag).

**[THE HISTORY TURN] wrong organ**
- Egyptian scene: a mummy, a brain being dropped into a trash can (nose-hook), while a heart sits carefully on a golden scale opposite a feather. Caption: "THEY THREW THE BRAIN AWAY."

**[HISTORY TWIST] the language fossils**
- Aristotle (toga) pointing proudly at a heart labeled "THINKING," with a sad little brain drawn as a dripping radiator. Cut: modern phrases float up around Stan, "LEARN BY HEART," "HEARTFELT," with a heart icon. Caption: "still in how we talk."

**[HUMAN LAYER] the reader**
- Stan in an armchair reading, while pages/thought-cards are handed UP to him from a dark basement hatch (his hidden mind churning below). Caption: "THE READER, NOT THE WRITER."

**[PAYOFF] you didn't write it**
- A nasty/ugly thought-bubble drifts toward Stan; he calmly lets it float past and plucks a different one to keep. Caption: "YOU DIDN'T WRITE IT. YOU CHOOSE WHAT TO KEEP."

**[BUTTON] lucky enough to be listening**
- Stan sitting calm, eyes a little wide with wonder, a single gentle thought arriving to him. Peaceful.
- Then: end card (main video) = silent like/subscribe doodle, LOWER-LEFT only, center + top-right CLEAR (brand/FORMATS.md). No CTA text on the main video.
