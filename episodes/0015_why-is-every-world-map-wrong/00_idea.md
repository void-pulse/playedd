# Episode Idea — Why Is Every World Map Wrong?
Date: 2026-06-10

- **Vein:** Everyday / Engineered (perception of the world). Rotates off the mind/existential run.
- **Working title:** Why Is Every World Map Wrong?
- **Hook (cold open):** The world map you grew up with is wrong. Greenland looks as big as Africa. Africa is 14 times bigger. And that's the small lie.
- **The reframe (it's not X, it's Y):** It's not a mistake mapmakers could fix. You cannot flatten a sphere without lying about something. Every flat map is a different lie; the only honest map of Earth is a globe.
- **EMOTIONAL REGISTER:** neutral + wonder, "huh, never realized." Light. Not cynical (it's geometry, not a conspiracy).
- **Verified facts (see 01_script.md SOURCES):** Mercator projection (Gerardus Mercator, 1569) made for SAILORS, preserves angles/compass bearings (straight line = constant heading) but distorts area, worse toward the poles; Greenland appears ~Africa-sized but Africa is ~14x larger; Alaska/Russia/Canada/Europe inflated; equatorial regions (Africa, India, South America) shrunk; Africa can contain the US + China + India + much of Europe; alternatives (Gall-Peters = equal area but stretched shapes; Robinson, Winkel tripel = compromises) all trade off something; no flat map is perfect; thetruesize.com demo.
- **MYTH-RISK:** don't call Mercator a "mistake" or conspiracy — it's a mathematically unavoidable tradeoff (sphere → flat). Don't crown any alternative as "the correct map" (all distort something; the globe is the only honest one). Keep ratios conservative (~14x Greenland/Africa).
