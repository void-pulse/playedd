# Storyboard notes — Your Brain Deleted Your First Years
# Rule (Logan): ONE visual punch every ~20-30s. People watch, not read. Stick-figure
# channels live on visual novelty. Several of these are deliberate "thumbnail moments."
# Style: doodle, but lean MOODY/high-contrast for the flagship (dark backgrounds, one
# dramatic light) like the ERASED thumbnail — a step up from the bright shorts look.

## Cold open
- Stan straining to remember, calendar pages "1st / 2nd / 3rd birthday" dissolving to blank.
- Filmstrip of Stan's life with the first frames torn out / white voids. (thumbnail moment)
- Stan as a baby glowing bright, then a hand "deletes" it — poof to blank.

## First memory is fake
- Stan proudly holding a memory photo; zoom in — it's pasted together from torn family photos.
- Piaget baby in a stroller, a shadowy "kidnapper," then the nanny pulling back a stage curtain — it was staged.

## The void
- Baby Stan clearly remembering: finds a hidden toy, says a word, knows a face.
- An adult Stan flipping a photo album — the early pages are pure white holes. (thumbnail moment)

## Brain under construction
- Tiny construction workers building roads inside a brain.
- A BULLDOZER literally flattening the old roads as new ones are laid. (Logan's idea, thumbnail moment)
- Footprints in cement being paved over.

## No one to remember it
- A house at night, lights ON, but an empty chair inside — nobody home. (Logan's idea)
- Baby Stan with no "I" speech bubble; older Stan with a glowing "I" appearing as words arrive.
- A filing cabinet with no name on the folder — nothing to file the memory under.

## The twist
- Two Stans side by side: a "different machine" — baby Stan as a wide-open sponge, adult Stan as a locked vault.

## Re-hook (the 5-min spike)
- Adult Stan staring at a baby photo with a giant red "?" — "was that ever really you?" (thumbnail moment)

## What you actually kept
- Erased scenes (blank film) BUT skills glowing: walking legs, a speaking mouth, a language symbol.
- Transparent toddler Stan glowing INSIDE the adult Stan. (Logan's idea, thumbnail moment)

## Why evolution does this
- Baby brain as a control room throwing all power to "LEARN PATTERNS," the "record episodes" dial at zero.
- A sponge soaking up a flood (language, faces, sound) while a tiny tape recorder sits unplugged.

## The person you'll never meet
- Baby Stan looking UP at giant parent figures.
- Adult Stan reaching back toward baby Stan, but a thread between them is cut.
- Final: a dark empty theater, one glowing screen playing a baby's first moments, adult Stan locked outside the door. (thumbnail moment) — hold, then black.

## Landing
- After the last line: ~2s black screen, then the channel outro card.
