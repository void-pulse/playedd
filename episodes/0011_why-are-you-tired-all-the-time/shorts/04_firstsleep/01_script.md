# ep11 short 4 — first sleep / second sleep (teaser)

[SHORT]

Waking up at three a.m. used to be completely normal.

For most of history, people slept in two shifts. A first sleep and a second sleep, with an hour wide awake in between.

They would get up around midnight, on purpose. Pray, talk, tend the fire.

Then one invention quietly deleted the gap. So if you wake at three a.m., maybe nothing is wrong with you at all.
